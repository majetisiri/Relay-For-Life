Relay For Life

Level 1

This level has 4 bulbs and each bulb has 2 switches respectively.
You have only one chance to switch on a bulb.
You will have to switch on 2 bulbs to reach level 2.

Level 2

This level has 3 bulbs and each bulb has 3 switches respectively.
You have only one chance to switch on a bulb.
You will have to switch on 2 bulbs to reach level 3.

Level 3

This level has only 1 bulb with 5 switches.
You have only one chance to switch on the bulb, if you succeed then you will win.
